Welcome to Athena Pali

Where Nature Meets Luxury

Premium 64-Acre Plotted Development

Athena Pali is a premium 64-acre plotted development crafted by PP Properties in the rapidly growing region of Pali, Maharashtra. Designed for those who value both nature and modern convenience, the community offers a peaceful escape with lush green landscapes, serene pathways, and beautifully planned open spaces.

What sets Athena Pali apart is its thoughtful balance between natural living and urban connectivity. From apple tree clusters perfect for weekend picnics to walking trails and leisure zones, every corner of this gated community is planned to help residents unwind and reconnect with nature. Yet, with the airport just 15 minutes away and top educational institutions like Harrow International School within a 5-minute drive, you enjoy unmatched access to key destinations without compromising on serenity.
